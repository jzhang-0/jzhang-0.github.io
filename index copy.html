Jin Zhang (张锦)

Research Engineer, Alibaba Group
Email: duchao0726 at gmail dot com

I am a research scientist at Sea AI Lab, Singapore. I finished my Ph.D. in 2019 at TSAIL Group in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Jun Zhu and Prof. Bo Zhang. And I received my B.Eng. from the Yao Class in Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences at Tsinghua University in 2014.

My research focuses on probabilistic learning and inference in machine learning (especially in deep learning), including deep generative models, Bayesian neural networks, general Bayesian inference and nonparametric methods.
